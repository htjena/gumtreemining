Services

Business Scope

At Gumtree Mining Pvt Ltd, we offer a wide range of services to meet your construction and mining needs. Explore our offerings below:

Construction Stones

Our extensive selection of construction stones includes various sizes and types to suit your specific needs. From sub-base materials to decorative aggregates, we have the right stone for every application.

Quarry Dust

Our quarry dust is a cost-effective solution for stabilizing soil, filling trenches, and enhancing the strength of concrete mixes. It is sourced from our own quarries and processed to meet rigorous quality standards.

Sand

Choose from our range of sands, including river sand and double washed sand, for superior performance in concrete, plastering, and landscaping projects. Our sands are washed, screened, and graded to ensure consistency and purity.

Pit Stand

Safety is paramount in mining operations, which is why we provide pit stand services to create secure working environments for our clients' teams. Our pit stands are designed to meet regulatory requirements and promote best practices in mine safety.

Contract Mining

Partner with us for comprehensive contract mining services tailored to your project requirements. Our experienced team, coupled with advanced equipment and technology, ensures efficient and cost-effective mining operations from exploration to production.

Earthmoving Equipment Hire

Access a diverse fleet of earthmoving equipment for rent, including excavators, bulldozers, loaders, and dump trucks. Our equipment is well-maintained, reliable, and operated by skilled professionals to optimize productivity on your site.
